About me

1995-2017 - Padova

I lived in Padova until the age of 22. I've been always good at math and I loved probability. Thus, I decided to enroll in the bachelor in Statistics at the University of Padova. During the university, I developed my passion for computational statistics and computer science. Thus I decided to enroll in the Master in Data Science. To support my studies during the weekend I was a football referee for the Italian Football Federation. In this period, had my first intership at Dina.corp. Diana is a start-up specialized in the management and promotion of e-commerce for luxe fashion brands.

2018 - Helsinki

In the second and last year of my Master, I attended the courses of the Master's degree in Data Science at the University of Helsinki. This experience has been a life changer. It was the first time I lived myself. I had the bliss to meet fantastic people that introduced me into the self-development word.

2019-2020 - Rome

I moved to Rome to work in big consulting company. I was lucky to work with magificent colleagies that help me to understand how datasicence projects are delopyed and matianed in production. During this period, I devloped my passion for art. On spare time I was a volounteer for international students in Rome

2020-2022 - Berlin

Helsinki left me the desire to live in a international evniroment, and strach my confort zone.

2022-Now - Prague

In May 2022, I decided to move again outside my confort zone and try to follow my dream in Prague.
